About Us

IEEE Women in Engineering (WIE) is one of the largest international professional organizations dedicated to promoting women engineers and scientists, and inspiring girls around the world to follow their academic interests in a career in engineering.Our goal is to facilitate the recruitment and retention of women in technical disciplines globally. We envision a vibrant community of IEEE women and men collectively using their diverse talents to innovate for the benefit of humanity.

Activities

Take a look through the activities we conduct to find new dimensions to women empowerment
Artisania
An online paper craft competition open to all across the nation organised by IEEE SB NSSCE in collabration with IEEE Bombay,Tamil Nadu and Kerala section as a joint initiative to fight against the pandemic by keeping the creative minds active.
Transliteration session
A webinar conducted by Women In Computing to give students a basic knowledge of transliteration and practical session to implement it.The session was handled by Subin Siby from Swanthanthra Malayalam Computing
Wie week celebrations
On the occasion of WiE week celebrations organised by Kerala section we actively involved as a team in conducting various events like open debate,continuous training session on PCB fabrication,science quiz etc
Xperion
As part of Xperion, the most prestigious four day flagship event of IEEE SB NSSCE, Women in Engineering Affinity Group conducted many sessions and activity programmes which were aimed at inspiring young women to come out of their comfort zone and to showcase their true potential.
LED Scrolling workshop
A three-week workshop with the initiative to involve girls of first and second year exclusively in making the LED scroller along with its installation in our IEEE SB room.Aimed at providing hands on experience and boosting practical knowledge
CTS Basic electronics workshop
First activity organised under continuous training session initiative which helped the WiE team to render hands on experience on basic electronics to junior girls and motivate them in having a practical approach while learning
CTS Basic electrical workshop
An activity organised under continuous training session initiative which helped the WiE team to render hands on experience on basic electrical to junior students and motivate them in having a practical approach while learning
Python workshop
A CTS aimed at teaching basics of programming using python making it a session comfortable to the beginners in programming and a revision class for those who have taken a long break from coding.
Android workshop
The workshop was conducted with the aim that students get familiarised with the process of Android development and to learn how new applications are created for devices running the Android operating system.

WiE IEEE

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. Our global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.
